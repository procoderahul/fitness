CHEST WORKOUT

THE TOP AND THE MOST EFFECTIVE EXERCISES TO BUILD YOUR CHEST ARE GIVEN BELOW.
THESE EXERCISES WILL HELP YOU BUILD YOUR CHEST IF YOU DO IT IN ITS CORRECT FORM OF DOING.
ALSO WE HAVE FOCUSED ON PROVIDING YOU SOME OF THE MOST COMMON MISTAKES THAT PEOPLE USUALLY DO IN THE GYM.
SOME COMMON TIPS ARE ALSO DISCUSSED AT THE END.

FLAT BENCHPRESS/ DUMBLEPRESS

  • üéØ: MEDIAL CHEST
  • SETS: 3
  • REP RANGE: 10-12
  • REST BETWEEN EACH SET: 45-60 SECONDS

This exercises is executed on a flat bench. You can use a dumbell or a barbell. You should have a grip of just outside the shoulder-width. You just need to have upside down motion with full flexion of hands while going up and squeeze the chest when the rep is completed. The most important point is to have the full range of motion.

INCLINE BENCHPRESS/ DUMBLEPRESS

  • üéØ: UPPER CHEST
  • SETS: 3
  • REP RANGE: 10-12
  • REST BETWEEN EACH SET: 45-60 SECONDS

This exercise can be performed by using an incline bench. You can either use a dumbell or a barbell for the same. The grip should be of just outside the shoulder-width. you need to position yourself properly and try to have the full upside-down motion. While going up you have squeeze your chest. Make sure when you take the motion down the barbell should land right in front pf your upper chest so that the upper chest may feel much more tension. The most important point is to have the full range of motion.

DECLINE BENCHPRESS/ DUMBLEPRESS

  • üéØ: LOWER CHEST
  • SETS: 3
  • REP RANGE: 10-12
  • REST BETWEEN EACH SET: 45-60 SECONDS

This exercise is performed on a decline bench. You can use either a dumbell or a barbell for the same. For safety ask a person to lift the barbell(if using) to prevent any injury. The grip should be of just outside the shoulder-width. You need to try to have the full upside-down motion. While going up you need to squeeze your upper chest. Make sure that when you bring the barbell/dumbell down it should land just above your lower chest so that the lower chest may feel more tension. The most important point is to have the full range of motion.

CABLE FLIES

  • üéØ: ENTIRE CHEST
  • SETS: 3
  • REP RANGE: 10-12
  • REST BETWEEN EACH SET: 45-60 SECONDS

You need to set the cable of both the sides according to the part you want to focus on.
::::::If you want to focus on the medial chest then place the cables parallel to your height and have the motion of flies.
::::::: If you want to focus on the upper chest place the cables at the extreme end and the motion of flies upwards to target your upper chest.
::::::: If you want to focus on you lower chest then place the cables at the extreme top and have the downward motion of the flies to target your lower chest.
This is one of the most effective exercises to build your chest muscles. The only thing is to have the correct measure of the weight that you have set up and having the full range of motion.

DUMBELL FLIES

  • üéØ: INNER CHEST
  • SETS: 3
  • REP RANGE: 10-12
  • REST BETWEEN EACH SET: 45-60 SECONDS

This exercise can be performed on any of the bench. You need to position yourself properly and have the full flies motion and make sure to squeeze your chest when bring the dumbell up so that the inner chest may feel more tension. Keep your elbows well positioned and tight to prevent any injury. The most important point is to have the full range of motion.

INCLINE HEX PRESS

  • üéØ: INNER CHEST
  • SETS: 3
  • REP RANGE: 10-12
  • REST BETWEEN EACH SET: 45-60 SECONDS

This is the most underrated chest exercise which we rarely see at the gyms. For performing this exercies you need have a neutral grip and have the full upside-down motion. You have to bring the dumbell paralell to your medial chest to activate your inner chest. Make sure that you have the ful range of motion.

SOME IMPORTANT TIPS

  • IN THE BENCHPRESSES, YOU SHOULD USE EITHER DUMBELL OR BARBELL BECAUSE THE MOTION IS SAME IN BOTH AND YOU WON'T GET ANY EXTRA BENEFIT OF DOING SO. YOU CAN PERFORM ALTERNATIVELY i.e. ONE DAY WITH DUMBELL AND OTHER DAY WITH BARBELL.
  • CHEST IS A BIG MUSCLE SO IF YOU CAN THEN REPEAT IT TWICE A WEEK.
  • TAKE THE LEAST POSSIBLE SUPPORT BECAUSE WHEN YOU TAKE THE SUPPORT YOU DONOT GIVE YOUR 100% EFFORT.
  • PLEASE DONOT LIFT MORE THAN YOUR CAPACITY AS IT CAN CAUSE A SEVERE INJURY.
  • DON'T DO TOO MANY EXERCISES OF THE SAME MOTION RATHER TRY DOING MORE EXERCISES OF DIFFERENT VARIATIN OF MOTION.
  • CORRECT FORM SHOULD BE THE TOP MOST PRIORITY.
  • WHILE PERFORMING FLIES MAKE SURE THAT YOUR ELBOWS ARE CORRECTLY POSITIONED AND WEIGHT IS NOT MORE THAN HEAVY AS IT CAN CAUSE INJURY TO YOUR ELBOWS.
  • THE MOST IS IMPORTANT THING IS SQUEEZING YOUR CHEST AND HAVING THE PROPER FULL RANGE OF MOTION
