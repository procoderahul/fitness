BACK WORKOUT

BACK IS ONE OF THE BIGGEST MuSCLES IN OUR BODY. THE MOST IMPORTANT POINT IS MAKING THE MIND-MUSCLE CONNECTION AS IT IS A MUSCLE WHICH WE CANNOT WATCH IN FRONT US LIKE THE REST OF THE MUSCLES. SO WHILE WORKING OUT ON THIS MUSCLE GROUP YOU HAVE TO BE FULLY CONCENTRATED AND FOCUSED SO THAT THE MUSCLE MIGHT GET TARGETTED.
THE TOP AND THE MOST EFFECTIVE EXERCISES TO BUILD YOUR BACK ARE GIVEN BELOW.
THESE EXERCISES WILL HELP YOU BUILD YOUR BACK IF YOU DO IT IN ITS CORRECT FORM OF DOING.

DEADLIFT

  • SETS: UPTO YOUR MAX STRENGTH
  • REP RANGE: 10-12(WHEN LIGHTWEIGHT) 3-5(IF HEAVY)
  • REST BETWEEN EACH SET: 45-60 SECONDS

One of the most difficult exercises to practise is known as deadlift. Its definition is hidden in its name. It is the most effective exercise that helps you work on your entire back especially lower back. One should definitely add this in their back day. There are a lots of things to focus on while doing the deadlift. For safety you should definitely wear a gym belt to prevent any injury. The most important part is having the correct posture as seen in the video giveen below. Lift only that much which you are capable of.

LAT PULLDOWN

  • SETS: 3-4
  • REP RANGE: 10-12
  • REST BETWEEN EACH SET: 45-60 SECONDS

A very effective workout that helps you bui;d your lats and upper back. It is done on the latpull machine which is available in every gym. The distance between the hands depends on the purpose of what you want to work on. The grip should be wide if you want higher mass in your back while the grip should be normal if you want to develop the v-shapern back. The most important point is maintaining the correct posture and having the full range of motion and squeezing your lats while coming down. One should add it in their back day.

ONE ARM DUMBELL ROW

  • SETS: 3
  • REP RANGE: 10-12
  • REST BETWEEN EACH SET: 45-60 SECONDS

This is one of the most effective back exercises that you should perform regularly in your back day. It mainly focuses on your side lats. It can be done in the position as shown below or you can also take the support of any equipment and perform while standing(bending). In important point to focus is having the full range of motion and squeeze the lats whike bringing the dumbell up to activate your side lats.

PULLUPS

  • SETS: 3
  • REP RANGE: 10-12
  • REST BETWEEN EACH SET: 45-60 SECONDS

This is an exercise that you can perform at your gym as well as your home. You just a hanging rod to perform it. It is also a very effective exercise that focuses on the entire back muscle group. To make it harder add some extra weights by tying them to your belt. This will add more weight to your body and make it harder to you to pull yourslef. You need to focus on the full range of motion of going complete down and pulling yourself up.

CABLE PULLLDOWN

  • SETS: 3
  • REP RANGE: 10-12
  • REST BETWEEN EACH SET: 45-60 SECONDS

To perform this exercise you can either use a rope or a straight rod. Place the cable at the extreme top and have the motion as shown in the video. Correct posture and correct technique of performing is the main key for this exercise. One should definitely add this exercise in their back day.

T-BAR ROW

  • SETS: 3
  • REP RANGE: 10-12
  • REST BETWEEN EACH SET: 45-60 SECONDS

A very effective to increase the muscle mass of the back msucle. It is peroformed by placing on end of the barbell at one end(open) and weights on one end. Make sure that the open end does not hinders. You have to hold the weighted end of the barbell and start rowing it as shown in the video. For saftey use belt to prevent any injury. The ain focus is maintaining the correct posture and having th right range of motion.
