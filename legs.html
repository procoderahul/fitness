LEGS WORKOUT

LEGS ARE SUCH A BODY PART ON WHICH THE ENTIRE WEIGHT OF YOUR BODY IS TRANSFERED THAT IS WHY THEY NEED TO BE VERY STRONG .
THE TOP AND THE MOST EFFECTIVE EXERCISES TO BUILD YOUR ABS ARE GIVEN BELOW.
THESE EXERCISES WILL HELP YOU BUILD YOUR ABS IF YOU DO IT IN ITS CORRECT FORM OF DOING.

SQUATS

  • SETS: 3-4
  • REP RANGE: 10-12
  • REST BETWEEN EACH SET: 45-60 SECONDS

This exercise can be performed using a barbell or even you can use the smith machine to perform it with the best form as the balance will not get disturbed.You just have to put the barbell on your shoulder/traps and start performing the normal squats as shown in the video below.

LUNGES

  • SETS: 2
  • REP RANGE: 10-12
  • REST BETWEEN EACH SET: 45-60 SECONDS

This is a very effective exercise to build your legs. You need to dumbells and a bench to perfrom it. You just to keep your one leg on the bench and the other staright on the floor. Then you have to perform lunges as normal as shown in the video given beloe.

LEG CURLS

  • SETS: 3-4
  • REP RANGE: 10-12
  • REST BETWEEN EACH SET: 45-60 SECONDS

This exercise is performed on the leg curl machine by just down in the nroaml position and start curling the machine. You just have to make sure that the form is correct and you are having the full range of motion. You can also train your hamstrings by lying reverse and then curling the legs. Both the thigh(front) and the hamstrings can be developed on this machine.

MACHINE LEG PRESS

  • SETS: 3-4
  • REP RANGE: 10-12
  • REST BETWEEN EACH SET: 45-60 SECONDS

This leg exercise is very effevtive to build your legs. This machine is needed to perform this exercise. You cab do this one in two ways. One as shown in the video and the other by laying down and having your legs stepped on the leg pas of this machine.
