EZ BAR CURL

  • üéØ : LONG HEAD
  • SETS: 3-4
  • REP RANGE: 10-12
  • REST BETWEEN EACH SET: 45-60 SECONDS

An EZ-bar curl is, well, easy. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, knees slightly bent. Hold an EZ-bar in an underhand grip with your arms extended and then curl the bar up towards your chest, keeping your elbows in to your sides. The most important thing is to maintain the correct form and have the full range of motion.

TRICEP WORKOUT

TRCIEP IS ALSO THE MOST IMPORTANT PART OF YOUR ARM TO MAKE IT LOOK GOOD. THE TRICEPS GIVE THE ROUND SHAPE TO YOUR ENTIRE ARM. IT IS A MUSCLE THAT CAN BE DEVELOPED EASILY IF YOU PERFORM THESE EXERCISES WITH FULL CONCENTRATION.
THE TOP AND THE MOST EFFECTIVE EXERCISES TO BUILD YOUR TRICEPS ARE GIVEN BELOW.
THESE EXERCISES WILL HELP YOU BUILD YOUR TRICEPS IF YOU DO IT IN ITS CORRECT FORM OF DOING.

CLOSE GRIP BENCHPRESS

  • üéØ : ENTIRE TRICEP
  • SETS: 3-4
  • REP RANGE: 10-12
  • REST BETWEEN EACH SET: 45-60 SECONDS

Grab a barbell with an overhand grip, keeping your index fingers on the inside edge of the knurling (i.e. the rough and textural part of the bar) Slightly arch your back to create minor space between your lower back and the bench. Life the bar off the rack and hold it above your sternum, keeping your arms completely straight and your elbows tucked at 45-degree angles on either side. Lower the bar until it lightly touches your body, pause, and then drive through at your feet as you press the bar upward. For the last set, consider reducing the weight and then increasing the number of reps, pushing yourself to failure.

ONE ARM ABLE PUSHDOWN

  • üéØ : LATERAL HEAD
  • SETS: 2
  • REP RANGE: 10-12
  • REST BETWEEN EACH SET: 45-60 SECONDS

This exercise is performed by using one arm at a time. Stand tall with your back to a cable machine, holding a D-handle in one hand. Keeping your chest up and core braced, press your hand forwards until your arm is straight. Reverse the move to the start and repeat for all the reps, then switch arms.

OVERHEAD TRICEP EXTENSIONS

  • üéØ : LONG HEAD
  • SETS: 3-4
  • REP RANGE: 10-12
  • REST BETWEEN EACH SET: 45-60 SECONDS

A very effective exercise to build your long head of the tricep. Sit perfectly upright on a workout bench and grab a dumbbell with one hand. Holding the dumbbell, lift your arm slightly behind your head with your elbow bent. Extend your elbow until your arm is straight overhead. Perform 10 reps with one arm before switching to the other to round out the set.

TRICEP DIPS

  • üéØ : ENTIRE TRICEP
  • SETS: 3-4
  • REP RANGE: 10-12
  • REST BETWEEN EACH SET: 45-60 SECONDS

A very effective body-weight exercise that can be performed by the following steps: Prop yourself up on the parallel bars, keeping your torso perpendicular to the floor. Maintaining your original position throughout, bend your knees and cross your ankles. Lower your body until your shoulder joints are below your elbows or just before you reach that position. Push back up to the starting position, until your elbows are just short of locked.
