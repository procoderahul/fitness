ABS WORKOUT

ABS/ ABDOMINAL IS A THE CORE OF YOUR BODY SO IT NEEDS TO BE VERY VERY STRONG SO THAT YOU MAY FEEL STRONG ALL THE TIME AND DO YOUR DAILY ACTIVITIES WITH EASE.
THE TOP AND THE MOST EFFECTIVE EXERCISES TO BUILD YOUR ABS ARE GIVEN BELOW.
THESE EXERCISES WILL HELP YOU BUILD YOUR ABS IF YOU DO IT IN ITS CORRECT FORM OF DOING.

SCISSOR LEG RAISES

  • SETS: 3-4
  • REP RANGE: 10-12
  • REST BETWEEN EACH SET: 45-60 SECONDS

This challenging but simple move is so effective ‚Äî and it specifically works the pesky lower ab area that can be difficult to work at times. You'll also take advantage of a nice quad exercise, which can help with running. Lie on your back with arms at your side. Pull navel into the spine. Lift both legs up at once until they are perpendicular to the floor. Slowly lower back down. Do not let back arch up off the floor. Add a scissor kick vertically and horizontally up to a few inches (legs stay straight and move up and down or side to side and crisscross each other).

FULL LEG RAISES

  • SETS: 2
  • REP RANGE: 10-12
  • REST BETWEEN EACH SET: 45-60 SECONDS

This is a very effective exercise to build your abs. to perform it: Lie on your back, legs straight and together. 2. Keep your legs straight and lift them all the way up to the ceiling until your butt comes off the floor. 3. Slowly lower your legs back down till they're just above the floor.

REVERSE CRUNCHES

  • SETS: 3-4
  • REP RANGE: 10-12
  • REST BETWEEN EACH SET: 45-60 SECONDS

This reverse crunches are like the normal crunches but a bit easier one . To perform it: Lift your feet off the ground and raise your thighs until they're vertical. Keep your knees bent at 90 degrees throughout the movement. Tuck your knees toward your face as far as you can comfortably go without lifting your mid-back from the mat. Your hips and lower back should lift off the ground.

SIDE PLANK KNEE KICKS

  • SETS: 3-4
  • REP RANGE: 10-12
  • REST BETWEEN EACH SET: 45-60 SECONDS

This abs exercise is very effevtive. you just have to lean down in the plank position straight. Now you kick your knees towards your abs and then bring it back doing this alternatively to contract and expand your abs.

V-CRUNCHES

  • SETS: 3-4
  • REP RANGE: 10-12
  • REST BETWEEN EACH SET: 45-60 SECONDS

A very effective exercise to develop the abdominal muscle. To perform it: Start by lying down on a mat. Keep your upper body on the floor and lift your legs straight up toward the ceiling. Slowly lower your legs, keeping your core engaged, until they are hovering about 6 inches above the ground. Hold here for 10 seconds. Return to the starting position and repeat.
